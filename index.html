i’m a front-end developer based in glasgow. i design and build websites for small businesses and creatives with a friendly and simple process.

i love helping people bring their ideas to life online — whether that’s a brand-new site or a refresh of something that’s been sitting untouched for a while. my focus is on creating websites that feel calm, clear and easy to use, with a bit of personality too.

if you’ve got an idea in mind (or no idea where to start), i’ll help you figure out what makes sense for you and your business.
